# Weekly campaign brief

Focus this week on game top-up campaigns that can make the search-to-SKU flow feel useful and easy to understand.

Prefer publishers with a manually provided promotion signal. If multiple publishers qualify, prioritize a recognizable game/store experience and strong search or purchase demand.

Creative direction:

- Keep banner copy short and consumer-friendly.
- Use the campaign discount exactly as provided by the promotion signal.
- Recommend popular search chips that help users reach the promoted publisher quickly.
- Do not invent external partnerships, event dates, or promotion terms that are not in the input files.
